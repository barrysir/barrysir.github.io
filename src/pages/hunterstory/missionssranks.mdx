Here is a list of every mission, including the points you need for an S-Rank, how much GP you need to unlock a mission, a rough scaling of the difficulty of the mission (which is actually generated based on the logarithm of the points), and a personal rating of how effective the map is for grinding (5/10 is average).

Mission score is awarded per arrow, based on the hang time and the distance of the mob away from you when the arrow hits.
The total score is then calculated with this formula:
  
`missionscore x accuracy x repelrate / 1000 = totalscore`
