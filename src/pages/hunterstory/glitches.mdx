Here's a list of all the glitches I've heard of.

#### <u>Flickering Buttons (Must be on downloaded version)</u>

If you expand the window beyond its usual dimensions, around the edges of the screen, you can see several buttons flashing red and black rapidly. They're clickable, but they don't do anything other than look nice.
