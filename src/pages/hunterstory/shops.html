For all secret bows, you need the previous bow to unlock the next one. Like you need Mega Ultimate Bow to buy the Bow of Lost Souls.

Also, for the Bow of Lost Souls, if you bought the Mega Ultimate Bow at 50 AAAs, then you do another mission, and you should get it.

The Curiosity Shop

The Curiosity Shop is a shop within the main Shop that is unlocked at Level 30.
There are 5 things you can buy in The Curiosity Shop:
 Item Cost Description
 SP Reset  4200*(level)+126000 mesos  Resets all your SP.
 Fortune Cookie  1 meso  Gives you various fortunes.
  •  You will find fortune in unexpected places.
  • A shining sun signals opportunity.
  • Secrets may hide in the shadows.
  • Today a dark-skinned stranger will defame you.
  • Stay true to yourself and fortune will follow.
  • Go nowhere, and let no monster stand in your way.
  • Misfortunes will befall you like rain.
  • Love is like a 10% scroll: it works 10% of the time.
  • You will become (slightly) richer. (+10 mesos)
  • You will become (slightly) more experienced. (+10 EXP)
  • You are about to have a VERY bad day. (unlocks The Call of Zakum)
 Stat Sheet  1234 mesos  Gives you a sheet of your stats, like how many points you have, how many arrows fired, how much damage dealt, etc.
 Ascendence Test  99,999,999 mesos  If you pass the test, you get to ascend. See the Hunterstory Guide for details.
 Score Percentage  9,999,999 mesos (after ascent)  Shows you some stats for your scores in missions. There's % of S-Rank, the regular point count, and a mysterious %S button.
