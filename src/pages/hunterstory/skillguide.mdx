<u><font size="4">Skill Guide</font></u>  
<u>  
</u>  
<u>A few terms needed to know:</u>  
(Keep Going) - Keep Adding SP into that skill until next job  
(MPR - x) - Add SP until that skill costs you MP Recovery/sec - x to use.  
Repeat - Repeat the series of highlighted moves until next job.  
Repeat until ____ - Repeat until the _____ part is fulfilled.  
(skill name)(x) - the x stands for what level it should be.
