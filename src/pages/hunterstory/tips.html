Tips

Always buy the last available bow. As in, if you can afford War Bow, buy it instead of Sport Bow.
Be patient. Ascends take a while.
Add 1 skill point into all active skills (that you want), then max out the passives.
Can't S-rank a mission? Here's what you do:
1. Get one point in B-55 SPIKE. The spiking will give you lots of extra points.
2. Enter the mission. Charge your shot, and move your cursor to the "100%" is in "Power: 100%".
3. Fiddle around until you get the perfect timing: when a monster shows on your screen, you let the arrow fly, and it hits the monster in the middle of its head.
4. Repeat Steps 1 - 3, until you have S'ed the mission. S ranking gets much easier as you gain more ascents, and as you play Hunterstory more.
Always kill the hardest boss that you can kill for EXP, and do the levels with huge spawn rates. (thanks!)
