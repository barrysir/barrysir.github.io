Cheats

Before anything: make sure you're showing hidden files and folders.

Windows Instructions
Go to Control Panel, and open up "Folder Options".
In the window that appears, select the View tab, and click "Show hidden files, folds, and drives".

This is only required for Savefile Hacking, but you will need to see hidden files and folders for many things involving changing something on your computer, so I'd recommend doing it anyways.

Speedhacking
Grinding in Hunterstory goes by quicker if you use a speedhack to speed up the game.
In my experience, 10x speed in Cheat Engine is the best setting.

Savefile Hacking
First, download .sol editor:
http://sourceforge.net/projects/soleditor/

Go to My Computer and follow the following paths, depending on your operating system:

Windows 7: C:/Users/(user)/Appdata/Roaming/Macromedia/Flash Player/#SharedObjects/(random numbers and letters)/flowsion.net***
Windows XP: C:/Documents and Settings/(user)/Application Data/Macromedia/Flash Player/#SharedObjects/(random numbers and letters)/flowsion.net***
Linux/Macs: home/(user)/.macromedia/Flash_Player/#SharedObjects/(random numbers and letters)/flowsion.net***
And open keylog1337.sol, which is somewhere within the folder, with .sol editor.

Now you can hack your Hunterstory save file! Here are some important values that should show up:
Level - Level
EXPTotal - Total EXP
TotalGP - Total GP
Badge__ (1-86, set it as a value) - Says whether or not the XXth badge has been unlocked
Mesos - Mesos
Bowwindup - Time taken to get to 100% power
MP, MPRecov - Max MP and MP recovery
watk - Weapon Attack, affects damage
dex - Dexterity, affects damage
skillpoints - Number of skill points

*** - If you play on a HunterStory flash, go to localhost instead. If you play on kongregate, go to either chat.kongregate.com, or kongregate.com. It's in one of those.
