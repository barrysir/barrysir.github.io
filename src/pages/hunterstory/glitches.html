Glitches

I will list every single Hunterstory glitch I have found here.

Flickering Buttons (Must be on downloaded version)
When you download Hunterstory V1.27A, you can see all these buttons which flash red and black rapidly.

Skill Boxes
Occasionally, when one hibernates their computer, and then turns it back on,all the skill boxes go flying to a random position.

The 80/100 Crash
This happens occasionally when one hibernates their computer. It comes from the same unknown cause as the Skill Boxes glitch.
It is called the 80/100 Crash because when you attempt a mission, your EXP is always 80/100, and you can't shoot anything.
To fix it, reopen Hunterstory.

It is possible to force it, by speedhacking the game to 0 speed.
